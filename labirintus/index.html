Labyrinth game guide

Basic things

You can navigate beetween the menu points by pressing 'w'/↑ and 's'/↓ , to choose from them press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.

You can choose form 2 languages and from 5 colors.

The game will ask you to load a map. Press 'Enter' if you are on this window to continue.

Write the name of the map after the 'File name:'. After that press 'Enter'.

You will be able to choose difficulty. The difficulty has impact on how many steps can you make maximum on the map.

After you start the game and you are on the map you can move with the 'w','a','s','d' keys or the ↑ , ← , ↓ , → keys on your keyboard.

  • Press 'w'/↑ to move up.
  • Press 'a'/← to move left.
  • Press 's'/→ to move down.
  • Press 'd'/↓ to move right.

Treasure rooms

There are treasure rooms on the map. You can acquire the treasure if you just need to enter to the room. The game will count you the amount of tresures you obtained in the up right corner.

How to save the map

Press the 'Esc' key on your keyboard and you will see a menu.

If you choose the option 'Save' the program will ask you to enter a name for the save.
